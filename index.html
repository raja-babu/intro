MY STORY


Hi...Hope you are breezing along this fine day. This is Raja Babu , a vivacious and fun loving soul. I was born and bred in a small town ,Gaya (India) but have since been through everything , from serenity and relatively slow life in Gaya to excruciating pace of Delhi to happiest metro city Kolkata.


I have a nuclear family consisting myself, my younger sister and my parents. Being brought up in a semi-urban society , they have a stoical mindset , never seen them complaining about anything. Working hard to fulfill all our needs. In midst of all the middle class family drama revolving around us daily , they have managed to teach us core life skills.


My childhood gyrated around two points : schools in the morning , and roaming around or playing cricket in the evening. I was an ingenious learner. My life suddenly altered completely at 14 when i was shortlisted for a scholarship program by a prestigious organisation. That took me to Delhi where I spent my next 4 years. What I learnt there was not mere academia. Delhi detour taught me to live independently , work in groups , coalesce with people of different backgrounds , culture,religions.


My next sojourn , where i am currently based ,is the city of joy Kolkata. I am pursuing B.E from Jadavpur University in Information Technology . My love with programming began here. Now I expend various hours sitting ,indulged into the world of coding. I also got to know the various technicalities related to make a PC work. I am expecting to enjoy rest of my life knowing the details of ever expanding digital world.


All these years, I have learnt many things. I have worked with a social welfare group NAVPRAYAS, working for education improvement in and around our society. I have actively participated in technical and cultural fest in my college as a volunteer, and all these experiences improved my social skills , to work in a co-ordinated group, my communication skill.


All in general, I live my life moment to moment enjoying every bit of it. I am an omnivorous reader , like listening to soothing music , and surf around internet. And if you are a philosophically inclined person , i am a garrulous preacher on that.....


I think i have introduced myself enough, to know more about me ... I guess sitting with me over a cup of coffee will do ...!!!
